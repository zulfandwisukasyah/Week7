Po (Kung Fu Panda)

Po, born as Lotus Shan and adopted as Po Ping, is the titular main protagonist of the Kung Fu Panda franchise. He is a Chinese giant panda who is one of Master Shifu's students, Mr. Ping's adoptive son, and Li Shan biological son. He is also believed to be the Dragon Warrior, the one destined to bring a new era of kung fu. For films and Dragon Knight, he is voiced by Jack Black, who played Dewey Finn from School of Rock, R.L.
